About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in computer science at Stanford University, advised by Professor Maneesh Agrawala. My research interest lies at the intersection of computer graphics and HCI. For my Ph.D., I build new media authoring tools to achieve editing goals motivated by human perception and attention. In 2015, I received a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences from UC Berkeley. At UC Berkeley, I did research in geometric modeling with Professor Carlo H. Séquin. I am currently funded by the Stanford Institute for Human-Centered AI (HAI).

About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in computer science at Stanford University, advised by Professor Maneesh Agrawala. My research interest lies at the intersection of computer graphics and HCI. In 2015, I received a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences from UC Berkeley. At UC Berkeley, I did research in geometric modeling with Professor Carlo H. Séquin. I am currently funded by the Brown Institute for Media Innovation.
